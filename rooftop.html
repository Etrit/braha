Overview

Our house is located in the beautiful neighbourhood of Prizren called Dardania, a quiet place with very clean air and breeze during the summer. Every part of the house is built with a lot of love, the house is completely new with the possibility of parking the car in front of the house. This attic apartment is an ideal vacation spot for a family or friends of 4 people. The apartment in the attic is about 70 m2 usable spaces, the bedroom connected to the large living room with complete kitchen, bathroom, TV, and all amanities you will need you can find here, also a very large terrace of 100 m2 with fantastic city view and Pashtrik Mountain. You can use this terrace for a pleasant evening dinner or barbeque with your family or friends in a perfect environment. We will be pleased to welcome you to our apartments.
