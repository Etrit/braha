Dardania Luxury Apartments

    Our house is located in the beautiful neighborhood of Prizren called Dardania, a quiet location with very clean air and fresh air during the summer. Every part of the house is built with a lot of love, the house is completely new with the possibility of parking the car in front of the house. An ideal vacation spot for a family of 4 to 6 people.
